Randomize Planet

Images of Earth can have the power to inspire reflection and action. From the iconic Blue Marble of Apollo 17 to the Pale Blue Dot of Voyager 1, technological advances have encouraged us to see our planet from a different perspective and focus on a core concept: impermanence.

Our planet is dynamic and always changing; with atmospheric and oceanic cycling, erosion, aridification, reforestation, etc., nothing is permanent. Humans understand this from images generated by satellite sensors and from scientific simulations of the Earth system.
